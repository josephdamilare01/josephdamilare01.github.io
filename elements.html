Providing Evidence of Online Networking's Role in Political Education: A Case Study of INEC X Handle

This study investigates the active role of online networking in political education, focusing on its benefits in terms of participation, interactivity, and cost-effectiveness. The aim of this study is to examine the degree to which students at Bayero University, Kano, utilise online networking for political education and to assess their awareness of INEC's usage of Twitter for political education.
